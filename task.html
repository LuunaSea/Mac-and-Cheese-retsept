Mac and Cheese
Ingredient Amount
Macaroni 400 g
Water 4 l
Butter 4 sl
Flower 1 dl
Milk 5 dl
Cheese 200 g
Salt and Black pepper pinch
  1. Step 1
    2. Boil macaroni in salted water until al dente, drain.
  2. Step 2
    3. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a pot. Whisk in the flour and heat while stirring for 1 minute. Add milk, whisk carefully. Bring the sauce to a boil and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Step 3
    4. Take the pot off the heat, stir in the pepper. Add grated cheese, stir until melted. Season with salt if necessary.
  4. Step 4
    5. Mix the sauce and pasta together and serve immediately.
